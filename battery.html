Battery

       The battery in the Tesla model x is one of the best batteries out there. It is known as the Lithium-ion battery type which is commonly known as being used in phones, laptops, tablets,speakers etc. They come in many variatys however the ones in the Tesla model x are extremley powerful to power the car itself. The man who invented this lithium-ion battery is John Bannister Goodenough there first commercial use was with Sony in 1991. This solved many issues for technology as it is rechargable, fast, powerful, and slim. The Tesla model X comes in two battery varieties the 75 kW-h under the AWD 90D package and the 95 kW-h AWD P90D package. The P90D package is capable of reaching 0-60 mph wotjom 3.2 seconds and 1/4 of a mile in 11.6 seconds, this enabling it to be the fastest vehicle in the suv class and also being able to go faster than a few racing sports cars. These batteries are capable of traveling 250 - 257 miles on a single charge it also allows for 250-500 hp in the front and rear wheels. This also allows you to only be charged 650-700 dollars for travelling aproxamitley 15,000 miles. This is great for the enviroment and the wallet however it does come with some advantages and disadvantages.
